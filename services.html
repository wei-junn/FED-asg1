Tyre Repair & Replacement Service

Experience safer, smoother rides with our professional tyre repair and replacement services. From patching punctures to fitting brand-new tyres, we ensure your bike performs at its best.

What It Involves:

  • Assessment: We inspect your tyres for wear, punctures, or sidewall damage and identify potential issues.
  • Puncture Repair: High-quality patches and sealants are used to fix minor leaks and restore functionality.
  • Tyre Replacement: When necessary, we replace worn-out tyres with durable, high-performance options tailored to your needs.
  • Wheel Alignment: Rims are checked and adjusted for compatibility and balance.
  • Pressure Optimization: Tyres are inflated to the ideal pressure for grip, efficiency, and durability.

Benefits:

  • Enhanced ride safety with optimal traction and stability.
  • Improved performance and reduced rolling resistance.
  • Cost-effective maintenance, extending the life of your tyres.
  • Smoother, more comfortable rides on all terrains.

Drivetrain Repair & Maintenance

Keep your bike’s drivetrain running smoothly with our professional repair and maintenance services. From chain replacements to gear adjustments, we’ve got you covered.

Benefits:

  • Improved pedaling efficiency and performance.

  • Smoother and more reliable gear shifting.
  • Enhanced cycling experience across all terrains.

Frame Repair & Painting

Revive your bike’s appearance and structural integrity with our frame repair and painting services. We ensure your bike is as durable as it is beautiful.

What It Involves:

  • Structural Inspection: Checking for cracks, dents, and weak points in the frame.
  • Frame Repair: Reinforcing or repairing damaged areas to restore safety and functionality.
  • Painting and Coating: Applying a fresh coat of durable, weather-resistant paint.
  • Customization: Optional personalized designs or colors for a unique look.

Benefits:

  • Enhanced frame durability and safety.
  • A refreshed and professional appearance for your bike.
  • Protection against rust and environmental wear.
  • Increased bike longevity and resale value.
